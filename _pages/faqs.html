You seem to be struggling. There's a pretty good chance that once you get into the conference you will discover a multitude of conference-focused activities, which may or may not include:

  • Meeting people
  • Attending talks, tutorials, or workshops
  • Visiting booths setup by our sponsors. Please don't be weird about it.

You can always leave the conference if it's not your thing. If you need help operating the door, please find an adult.
